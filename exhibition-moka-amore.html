About this event

Exhibit Dates: April 19-21, 2025

An exquisite exhibition of iconic Italian coffee makers mapping their history, unique geometry, and cultural worth.

Collector & Curator

About the Curator

Louie Chiaino, Curator & Collector of Moka Amore. A true collector of Mokas, Louie is bewitched by the contours of each treasured piece. He is fascinated by its history and enchanted with its cultural worth.

He scours vintage design sites, second-hand stores, front lawns, and strangers' garages in quest of new gems to add to his bevy of beauties.

"The Moka is more than just Nonna's coffee pot—it is a geometric marvel, an icon of design, and a vessel of warm Italian memories."

— Louie Chiaino
