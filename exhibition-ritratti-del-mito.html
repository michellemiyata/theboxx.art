Mixed-Media Showcase

EOS Ritratti del Mito

Classical portraits and ancient mythology on plywood

About the Show

EOS Ritratti del Mito is an evocative mixed-media showcase exploring mythology, classical portraits, and contemporary textures. The artworks are composed of mixed-media applications on wood plywood (compensato), highlighting classical silhouettes within modern, distressed surfaces.

Curated and featuring works by Andrew Abballe and other contemporary Canadian classicists, this exhibition bridges historical themes with contemporary material choices.

"Classical myths are not dead letters; they are frameworks for modern identities. Working with wood allows the grain to dictate the portraits' wrinkles and history."

— Andrew Abballe, Co-Curator
