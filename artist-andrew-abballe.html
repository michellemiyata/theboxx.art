Andrew Abballe

Oil & Mixed Media Painter

Biography

Andrew Abballe is a rising Canadian artist whose work bridges time-honored oil painting traditions with a distinctly modern, street-level flair. Inspired by classical realism, baroque emotionalism, and urban pop textures, Andrew crafts deep mixed-media compositions that explore memory, identity, and the passing of time.

Using layers of gesso, gold foils, and classical pigments on canvas and wood panel substrates, Andrew's paintings reflect structural depth and material resilience. His practice forces the classical masters into dialogue with contemporary Canadian expressions.

"My practice is a collision of reverence and rebellion—respecting the master techniques of classical oil painting while carving a lane that represents the raw emotions of our current generation."

— Andrew Abballe
